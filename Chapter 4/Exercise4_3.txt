All are valid except for these below:

0x10.5	- Decimals are not allowed in hexadecimal constants.

0X0G1	- What is even G?

98.7U	- Unsigned variable must be an integer

17777s	- s is not allowed and does not mean anything there.

0996 - Beginning 0 signifies octal constant value, 9 is not within acceptable range (0-7).

1.2Fe-7 - F does not belong in expression.

15,000	- Cannot use ,(comma) for numbers greater than 999.
